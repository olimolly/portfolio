## Overview

- Progressive consolidation of business tools within the Efficy CRM, integrated with Laravel.
- Data migration following a layered approach (bronze, silver, gold).
- Front-end integration with Vue.js and build tooling managed with Vite.
- Data management and optimization using PostgreSQL.
- Integration and synchronization with the Efficy CRM.
